WHY FIGHT FOOD WASTE

Together we can make the difference

Around 88 million tonnes of food are wasted annually in the EU, and over 40% occurs at retail and consuler level. Wasting food depletes the enviroment of limited natural resources and is also a waste of money.It depends of every one of us to do our small part in preventing and reducing food waste.

The misunderstandings of the "best before" and "use by" date labels leading to edible foods being thrown away but a wide range of initiatives to prevent and reduce food waste can be taken. If everyone start at local level to implement some measures, then it will make a big difference to the world.
